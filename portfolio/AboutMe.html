ABOUT ME

I'm Nathaniel Dodson, a junior-year Software Engineering student at BYU–Idaho with a strong passion for games, systems, and software design. I’ve always been fascinated by how games work under the hood, especially the mechanics and design principles that make them engaging. While I hope to work in the game development industry someday, I'm equally excited by opportunities in broader software fields that involve creative problem-solving and system design.

I’m most comfortable in Python and C#, and have experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I’ve also worked with Java, C++, SQL, and engine-specific languages like GDScript (Godot).

My projects range from gameplay prototypes to scripting tools and utilities, several of which are featured on the Projects page of this site.

Whether it’s building interactive systems or refining user experiences, I love the challenge of turning ideas into something real and functional.
